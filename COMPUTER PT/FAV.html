Cafe Latte

My favorite drink, the Cafe Latte is a coffee-based drink made primarily from espresso
and steamed milk. It consists of one-third espresso, two-thirds heated milk and about 1cm of foam.
Depending on the skill of the barista, the foam can be poured in such a way to create a picture.
